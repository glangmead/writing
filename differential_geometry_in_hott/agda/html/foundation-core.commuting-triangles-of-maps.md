## Idea

A triangle of maps

```text
        top
     A ----> B
      \     /
  left \   / right
        V V
         X
```

is said to **commute** if there is a [homotopy](foundation-core.homotopies.md)
between the map on the left and the composite of the top and right maps:

```text
  left ~ right ∘ top.
```

     \   /
      V V
       X

commutes.
