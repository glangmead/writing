## Idea

Given a map `f : A → B` and an element `b : B`, the **fiber** of `f` at `b` is
the preimage of `f` at `b`. In other words, it consists of the elements `a : A`
equipped with an [identification](foundation-core.identity-types.md) `f a ＝ b`.
