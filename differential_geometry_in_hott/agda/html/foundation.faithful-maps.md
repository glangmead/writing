## Idea

Since we sometimes think of types as ‚àû-groupoids, with the groupoid structure
provided implicitly by the identity type and its induction principle, we can
think of maps as functors of ‚àû-groupoids. We borrow some terminology of
functors, and call a map faithful if it induces embeddings on identity types.
