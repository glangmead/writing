## Idea

A **retraction** is a map that has a right inverse, i.e. a section. Thus,
`r : B → A` is a retraction of `f : A → B` if the composition `r ∘ f` is
homotopic to the identity at `A`. Moreover, in this case we say that `A` _is a
retract of_ `B`.
