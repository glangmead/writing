## Idea

An **extension** of a map `f : (x : A) → P x` along a map `i : A → B` is a map
`g : (y : B) → Q y` such that `Q` restricts along `i` to `P` and `g` restricts
along `i` to `f`.

```text
  A
  |  \
  i    f
  |      \
  v       v
  B - g -> P
```
